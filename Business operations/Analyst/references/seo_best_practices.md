# SEO Best Practices for US B2B Content

## Core Ranking Factors (2024-2025)

### 1. E-E-A-T Signals
- **Experience**: First-hand industry knowledge, case studies, real project examples
- **Expertise**: Author credentials, technical accuracy, industry terminology
- **Authoritativeness**: Backlinks from industry sites, mentions, citations
- **Trustworthiness**: Contact info, certifications (UL, NEC), business verification

### 2. Search Intent Alignment
| Intent Type | User Need | Content Structure |
|------------|-----------|-------------------|
| Informational | Learn/understand | How-to, guides, explanations |
| Commercial Investigation | Compare/evaluate | "Best X", reviews, comparisons |
| Transactional | Buy/contact | Product pages, pricing, contact forms |

### 3. On-Page SEO Essentials
- **Title Tag**: 50-60 chars, primary keyword near start
- **H1**: Single, clear, matches search intent
- **H2-H6**: Logical hierarchy, include secondary keywords
- **Meta Description**: 150-160 chars, include CTA
- **URL Structure**: Short, descriptive, keyword-rich
- **Internal Links**: 3-5 relevant links to related content
- **Image Alt Text**: Descriptive, include keywords naturally

### 4. Content Depth Benchmarks
- **Short-form Blog**: 800-1,200 words
- **Medium-form Guide**: 1,500-2,500 words
- **Comprehensive Resource**: 3,000+ words
- **Landing Page**: 500-1,000 words (above fold matters more)

### 5. Readability Standards
- **Flesch Reading Ease**: 60-70 (8th-9th grade level)
- **Sentence Length**: 15-20 words average
- **Paragraph Length**: 3-4 sentences max
- **Subheadings**: Every 300 words

### 6. Technical SEO Checklist
- [ ] Mobile-responsive design
- [ ] Page load speed < 3 seconds
- [ ] HTTPS enabled
- [ ] Schema markup (Article, FAQPage, Organization)
- [ ] Clean URL structure
- [ ] XML sitemap updated
- [ ] No broken links

### 7. US B2B-Specific Signals
- Local business schema
- "Made in USA" / "US-based" mentions
- Industry certifications prominently displayed
- Contact information visible
- Business hours clearly stated
- Physical address (if applicable)

## Keyword Research Framework

### Primary Keyword Selection Criteria
1. **Search Volume**: 100+ monthly searches (US)
2. **Keyword Difficulty**: < 50 for new content
3. **Commercial Intent**: Match to business goals
4. **Relevance**: Direct connection to BASIS SIGN offerings

### Keyword Placement Density
- **Primary Keyword**: 0.5-1% density
  - Title tag (1x)
  - H1 (1x)
  - First 100 words (1x)
  - Throughout body naturally
  
- **Secondary Keywords**: 3-5 variations
  - H2s (2-3x)
  - Body content naturally
  
- **LSI Keywords**: 10-15 semantically related terms
  - Sprinkled throughout

## Content Structure Templates

### Blog Post Template (Informational)
```
H1: [Primary Keyword] - Complete Guide
├─ Introduction (100-150 words)
│  ├─ Hook/Problem statement
│  ├─ What reader will learn
│  └─ Brief credibility signal
├─ H2: What is [Primary Keyword]?
├─ H2: Why [Primary Keyword] Matters
├─ H2: How to [Do Something Related]
│  ├─ H3: Step 1
│  ├─ H3: Step 2
│  └─ H3: Step 3
├─ H2: Common Mistakes to Avoid
├─ H2: FAQ
└─ Conclusion + CTA
```

### Landing Page Template (Commercial)
```
H1: [Product/Service] - [Key Benefit]
├─ Hero Section
│  ├─ Value proposition
│  ├─ Primary CTA
│  └─ Trust badge
├─ H2: The Problem
├─ H2: Our Solution
├─ H2: Key Features/Benefits (3-5)
├─ H2: How It Works
├─ H2: Why Choose Us
├─ H2: Social Proof
└─ Final CTA
```

## Link Building Strategy

### Internal Linking Best Practices
- Link from high-authority pages to new content
- Use descriptive anchor text (not "click here")
- 3-5 contextual links per 1,000 words
- Link to conversion pages from informational content

### External Link Signals
- Industry publications
- Government resources (.gov)
- Educational institutions (.edu)
- Trade associations
- Manufacturing standards organizations

## Conversion Optimization

### CTA Placement Strategy
- **Above the fold**: Primary CTA for high-intent traffic
- **Mid-content**: Soft CTA (newsletter, download)
- **End of content**: Strong CTA for engaged readers
- **Sidebar/sticky**: Persistent secondary CTA

### Trust Elements to Include
- Certifications (UL, NEC, ISO)
- Years in business
- Number of projects completed
- Client logos
- Warranty/guarantee information
- Return policy
- Contact information

## Performance Metrics

### Track These KPIs
- **Organic Traffic**: Month-over-month growth
- **Ranking Position**: Top 3 for primary keyword
- **Click-Through Rate**: > 3% average
- **Time on Page**: > 2 minutes for blog posts
- **Bounce Rate**: < 60% for landing pages
- **Conversion Rate**: Track form submissions, calls, quotes

### Tools for Monitoring
- Google Search Console
- Google Analytics 4
- Ahrefs / SEMrush
- PageSpeed Insights
- Screaming Frog (technical audits)
