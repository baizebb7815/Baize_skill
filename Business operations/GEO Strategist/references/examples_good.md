## Key Takeaways Across Both Examples

### What Made These Pages Work

1. **Specificity over generalities**
   - âŒ "Fast installation"
   - âœ… "2-3 hours vs 2-3 days"

2. **Address objections proactively**
   - B2B: Permit concerns â†’ UL cert + documentation
   - B2C: Install anxiety â†’ Video + "no tools needed"

3. **Real numbers build trust**
   - "3,500+ restaurants" > "thousands"
   - "$1,200 saved" > "significant savings"
   - "4.9 from 840" > "highly rated"

4. **Quotable definitions for GEO**
   - Both pages included citation-worthy sentences
   - Structured for AI to summarize

5. **Multiple CTAs with different stakes**
   - High stakes: "Get Quote" (commitment)
   - Low stakes: "See Gallery" (exploration)
   - Medium stakes: "Free Mockup" (engagement)

6. **Authentic social proof**
   - Real names, specific locations
   - Actual outcomes and numbers
   - Customer photos where possible

7. **Risk reversal everywhere**
   - Warranties
   - Guarantees
   - Free mockups/guides
   - No-obligation language
