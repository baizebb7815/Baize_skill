# Product Matrix Reference

## Channel Letters

### Product Overview
Three-dimensional illuminated sign characters typically made from aluminum returns (sides) with acrylic faces. Most common signage type for storefronts, buildings, and commercial identification.

### Types

#### 1. Front-Lit (Standard)
**Construction:**
- Aluminum return (3"-5" deep typical)
- Translucent acrylic face (1/8" or 1/4" thick)
- LEDs mounted inside facing the face

**Illumination:** Face glows, sides dark
**Best for:** Retail, restaurants, general storefront use
**Visibility:** Excellent day and night

#### 2. Halo-Lit (Reverse Channel)
**Construction:**
- Aluminum return (4"-6" deep typical)
- Opaque acrylic or metal face
- LEDs mounted inside facing the wall

**Illumination:** Halo glow around perimeter, face dark
**Best for:** Upscale restaurants, corporate, boutique
**Visibility:** Lower daytime contrast, elegant night presence

#### 3. Front & Halo (Combination)
**Construction:**
- Combines both front-lit and halo-lit LEDs
- Requires deeper return (5"-7" typical)

**Illumination:** Both face and halo glow
**Best for:** Maximum visibility, high-end applications
**Visibility:** Best overall, premium positioning

#### 4. Raceway Mounted
**Construction:**
- Letters mounted on aluminum raceway (enclosure)
- All wiring hidden inside raceway
- Single electrical connection

**Installation advantage:** Easier permit approval, faster install
**Best for:** Multi-tenant buildings, permits required

### Key Selling Points (B2B)

**Labor Cost Savings:**
- Pre-assembled: 2-3 hours install time
- Traditional: 2-3 days on-site fabrication
- Savings: $800-1200 in electrician fees

**Code Compliance:**
- UL 48 listed components
- NEC compliant wiring
- Permit documentation included

**Installation Simplicity:**
- Mount backboard/raceway to wall
- Connect pre-wired letters
- Single electrical connection
- No on-site fabrication

### Specifications

**Standard Letter Heights:**
- 6", 8", 10", 12", 15", 18", 24", 30", 36"
- Custom sizes available

**Return Depths:**
- Standard: 3", 4", 5"
- Deep return: 6"-8" (for halo-lit)

**Mounting Options:**
- Flush wall mount (studs drilled into wall)
- Raceway mount (all letters on enclosure)
- Post/monument mount (ground-based)

**Materials:**
- Returns: Aluminum (powder-coated)
- Faces: Acrylic (translucent or opaque)
- LEDs: Typically 12V or 24V modules

## LED Neon Flex Signs

### Product Overview
Flexible LED tubing encased in silicone that mimics the look of traditional glass neon but with modern safety and efficiency.

### Construction
- Flexible LED strip
- Silicone extrusion (PVC alternative)
- Mounting on acrylic or metal backing
- Low-voltage power (12V typical)

### Types

#### 1. Wall-Mounted Neon
**Backing:** Clear or colored acrylic (1/4"-1/2" thick)
**Installation:** Standoffs create 1/2"-1" gap from wall
**Best for:** Indoor decor, business interiors, events

#### 2. Tabletop Neon
**Backing:** Weighted acrylic or wood base
**Installation:** Freestanding, plug-and-play
**Best for:** Weddings, parties, home decor

#### 3. Outdoor Neon
**Protection:** IP65 rated silicone, waterproof power
**Mounting:** Heavy-duty acrylic or aluminum backing
**Best for:** Outdoor events, patios, semi-permanent installs

### Key Selling Points (B2C)

**Safety First:**
- Low voltage 12V (vs 15,000V for glass neon)
- Cool to touch
- UL-certified power adapters
- Safe around kids and pets

**Foolproof Installation:**
- Plug-and-play system
- Mounting hardware included
- No tools required for most applications
- Video tutorials available

**Aesthetic Confidence:**
- Free design mockups
- Professional guidance
- Unlimited design revisions
- See-before-you-buy

### Specifications

**Neon Colors Available:**
- Warm White (3000K)
- Cool White (6000K)
- Red, Blue, Green, Yellow, Orange, Pink, Purple
- RGB (color-changing)

**Tubing Sizes:**
- 8mm (standard)
- 10mm (brighter, bolder)
- 15mm (maximum visibility)

**Power:**
- 12V DC standard
- UL-listed power adapter included
- Dimmer compatible
- Average consumption: 6-10W per foot

**Backing Options:**
- Clear acrylic (modern, minimal)
- Colored acrylic (adds depth)
- Wood (rustic, warm)
- Custom shapes available

## Metal Illuminated Letters

### Product Overview
Premium signage using brushed or polished metal faces with LED backlighting, creating a sophisticated halo effect. Common for corporate lobbies, law firms, medical offices.

### Construction
- 304 stainless steel or aluminum face (1/8" thick)
- Aluminum return (3"-5" deep)
- LED modules facing the wall
- Precision-cut finish options

### Finish Options

**Brushed Stainless Steel:**
- Most popular
- Professional, modern
- Fingerprint resistant

**Polished Stainless Steel:**
- Mirror finish
- High-end aesthetic
- Requires regular cleaning

**Powder-Coated Aluminum:**
- Color options available
- Matte or gloss finish
- Cost-effective alternative

**Bronze/Brass:**
- Warm metallic tone
- Premium positioning
- Traditional elegance

### Key Selling Points (B2B)

**Premium Positioning:**
- Elevates brand perception
- Communicates quality and permanence
- Justifies higher pricing

**Durability:**
- 20+ year lifespan typical
- Scratch and corrosion resistant
- Minimal maintenance required

**Professional Installation:**
- Requires mounting template
- Professional install recommended
- Precise alignment critical

### Specifications

**Standard Letter Heights:**
- 6", 8", 10", 12", 15", 18", 24"
- Logo sizes custom quoted

**Return Depths:**
- 3" (standard halo)
- 4"-5" (deeper halo, more dramatic)

**Mounting:**
- Stud mount (invisible fasteners)
- Floating mount (1/2"-1" standoff)
- Template provided for alignment

**Applications:**
- Corporate lobbies
- Law firms, medical offices
- High-end retail
- Luxury hospitality

## Light Boxes

### Product Overview
Illuminated cabinet signs with translucent face, typically rectangular. Common for wayfinding, menu boards, mall directories.

### Construction
- Aluminum frame/cabinet
- Translucent face (acrylic or polycarbonate)
- Internal LED lighting
- Graphics printed on vinyl or directly on face

### Types

#### Single-Face Light Box
- One illuminated side
- Wall-mounted typical
- Most common type

#### Double-Face Light Box
- Two illuminated sides
- Projecting/blade mount
- Visible from multiple angles

#### Ultra-Thin Light Box
- 1"-2" depth only
- LED edge-lighting
- Modern, minimal aesthetic

### Applications
- Wayfinding signage
- Menu boards (restaurants, cafes)
- Mall directories
- Transit signage
- Trade show displays
