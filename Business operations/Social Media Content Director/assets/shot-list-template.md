## Style Validation Checklist

Before generating AI prompts, verify:

- [ ] All shots use POV or hands-only perspective
- [ ] No faces mentioned in any shot
- [ ] Natural, realistic lighting described for each shot
- [ ] Camera movements are simple (static, slow push/pull)
- [ ] Backgrounds are simple and uncluttered
- [ ] American home/business environments specified
- [ ] Each shot has clear subject focus
- [ ] Total duration matches platform recommendation
- [ ] Text overlays are concise and readable
- [ ] Story arc is clear (hook → development → payoff)
