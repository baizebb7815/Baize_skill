## AÔ∏è‚É£ Installation Demos (Highest Trust)

**Why it works:** Shows product in action, reduces purchase anxiety, positions brand as helpful.

**Retention strategy:** "How easy is this?" hook ‚Üí clear steps ‚Üí satisfying completion.

### A1: "From Box to Wall" (30-second version)

**Structure:**
1. **Hook (0-3s):** Unopened box on floor
2. **Unboxing (3-8s):** Hands opening, revealing sign + mounting kit
3. **Prep (8-15s):** Marking wall, attaching bracket
4. **Install (15-25s):** Hanging sign, plugging in
5. **Reveal (25-30s):** Lit sign on wall, pull-back shot

**Text overlay examples:**
- "You won't believe how easy this is"
- "No electrician needed ‚úì"
- "Installed in under 3 minutes"

**Ideal for:** Instagram Reels, TikTok

---

### A2: "No Tools Needed" Installation (15-second version)

**Structure:**
1. **Hook (0-2s):** Text: "Wait, no tools?"
2. **Show (2-8s):** Hands peeling adhesive backing
3. **Stick (8-12s):** Pressing sign onto wall
4. **Reveal (12-15s):** Plugged in, glowing

**Text overlay examples:**
- "Damage-free installation"
- "Renter-friendly ‚úì"
- "3M adhesive backing"

**Ideal for:** TikTok, Pinterest

---

### A3: "One Person Install" (20-second version)

**Structure:**
1. **Hook (0-3s):** POV looking at large blank wall
2. **Measure (3-8s):** Hands using level/tape measure
3. **Mount (8-15s):** Screwing in bracket (close-up)
4. **Hang (15-18s):** Lifting sign onto bracket
5. **Light (18-20s):** Flipping switch, sign illuminates

**Text overlay examples:**
- "Did this myself in 10 minutes"
- "Weighs less than 3 lbs"
- "Secure bracket included"

**Ideal for:** Instagram Reels, Pinterest
