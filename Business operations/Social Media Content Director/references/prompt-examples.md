Proven, tested prompts for Sora 2, Veo 3, and similar AI video generation models. Organized by shot type and content category.

## üéØ Prompt Structure Template

Every effective AI video prompt includes:

```
[SHOT TYPE] + [SUBJECT/ACTION] + [CAMERA BEHAVIOR] + [LIGHTING] + [STYLE MODIFIERS] + [ENVIRONMENT]
```

**Critical elements:**
- **POV specification** (first-person view)
- **No faces** (hands-only)
- **Realistic style** (avoid "cinematic")
- **Clear action** (what's happening)
- **Simple movement** (static or slow)

## üì¶ A. Unboxing & Product Reveal Prompts

### A1: Basic Unboxing Shot

**Scenario:** Opening LED sign package

**Prompt:**
```
POV first-person view looking down at hands opening a cardboard shipping box on a wooden table. Hands lift the cardboard flaps to reveal a neon-style LED sign wrapped in protective bubble wrap. Natural window light from the left side, soft ambient indoor lighting. Semi-professional smartphone aesthetic, realistic home setting. Static camera, no movement. 5 seconds.
```

**Why it works:**
- Clear POV perspective
- Specific lighting direction
- Realistic home context
- No face, hands-only

---

### A2: Product First Look

**Scenario:** Revealing sign from packaging

**Prompt:**
```
Close-up POV shot of hands carefully peeling bubble wrap off an LED neon sign on a clean white desk. Fingers gently unwrap the protective film, revealing the smooth acrylic surface. Warm LED glow begins to show through translucent material. Natural daylight through window, slightly handheld feel. Realistic texture on hands and materials. 6 seconds.
```

**Why it works:**
- Tactile detail (peeling, unwrapping)
- Material textures specified
- Slow reveal creates anticipation

### B2: Drilling/Mounting Shot

**Scenario:** Installing mounting bracket

**Prompt:**
```
Close-up POV of hands operating a cordless drill, driving a screw into a white drywall with a small black metal mounting bracket. Slight camera shake from drill vibration, very realistic and organic feel. Workshop lighting, natural daylight mixed with overhead bulb. Focus on hands and drill, blurred background. **Why it works:**
```
- Action clearly described
- Realistic physics (drill shake)
- Appropriate depth of field

### B3: Hanging the Sign

**Scenario:** Placing sign on mounted bracket

**Prompt:**
```
POV shot from installer's perspective, looking at hands lifting a medium-sized LED sign and carefully sliding it onto a wall-mounted bracket. Both hands visible, guiding the sign into place with precision. Clean living room wall background, soft natural light from window to the right. Static camera with slow push-in toward the sign as it settles. Realistic, semi-professional smartphone video style. 6 seconds.
```

**Why it works:**
- Dual-hand coordination shown
- Environmental context included
- Gentle camera movement specified
